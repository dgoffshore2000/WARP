Curve Results

In the ​PLOT page:

Select the ​model path corresponding to your project. Choose the target results (e.g., RAO, wave forces) from the dropdown at the bottom. Click ​Apply to generate curve plots. Within the ​curve display interface:

​Save Plots: Export images.
​Further Operations: Adjust axes, zoom/pan, or overlay multiple datasets.
​​​ ​​​​

The curve visualization is powered by ​Matplotlib , a Python plotting library, ensuring flexible customization of plot styles and formats.
